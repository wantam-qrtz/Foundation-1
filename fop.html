What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, authoritative Word of God and the only rule for faith and conduct.

The Church

We believe in the universal Church, a living spiritual body of which Christ is the head and all regenerated persons are members.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone, not by works, and is available to all who believe.

Baptism

We believe in water baptism by immersion after repentance and acceptance of Christ as personal Savior.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Fellowship

We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ and the importance of fellowship.

Resurrection

We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost—the saved to eternal life and the lost to eternal separation from God.

Our History

The Foundation of Praise Ministries was founded on 4th June 2007. The founders of the ministry are Rev. Wilson Mujukane and his lovely wife Jessica Nakhungu. This ministry was born through a vision and prophecy shared to the servant of God two years before its inception.

The name of the Ministry was revealed to the servant of God through scripture; in the book of Psalms 11:3. The reading that elaborates, "If the foundations be destroyed, what can the righteous do?" Together with the early congregants that joined in the fellowship, the belief that the Lord has called us all to rebuild lives to all who seek refuge continues to impact and transform many that associate with the ministry. In celebration of praises in redemption, the name Foundation of Praise continues to complete testimonies as membership increases in number.

Our Journey

For many years, the church has witnessed transformational evolution of lives through developments and progress in; education, marriage, business, personal and community projects as well as spiritual nourishment. Many souls have been transformed, restored, mentored and nurtured through Biblical teachings, stories and real life experiences.

The Lord has been gracious and faithful in helping members contribute and donate throughout the course of church programs. With the same, the church has expanded over time as testimonies continue to grow.

Success

The church has expanded its dreams and objectives through impacts of many lives with aims to restore unity among community members. Through continuous fellowships, the church has promoted harmony, sense of belonging and ownership across diverse cultures across the nation. Therefore, the Foundation of Praise remains a proud refuge where everyone matters.

The church has so far established departments in categories that are active till date. They are; Family outreach, Sunday school ministry, Youth ministry, Ladies' ministry, Men's ministry and the Elder's ministry.

Future Plans

  • To train and empower and equip servants of God with necessary tools to win souls to Jesus Christ
  • To establish training Centers
  • To be a disciple making church in the community
  • To involve the government and community promote sustainable development goals

Rewards

"1 Corinthians 15:58: 'Therefore, my beloved brethren, be steadfast, unmovable, always abounding in the work of the Lord, forasmuch as ye know that your labour is not in vain in the Lord'. This verse assures believers that every act of service and faithfulness to God, even when it feels exhausting or unrecognized, will never be wasted—God sees and will reward every effort done for Him. In God's kingdom, your faithful labour is never in vain, for He promises to remember and reward all that is done for His glory."

Community Engagement

Our church is dedicated to the growth of our community not just in Word but also in deeds filled with love.

"And let us consider how we may spur one another on toward love and good deeds..." (Hebrews 10:24)

This scripture reminds us that our faith is not just about personal growth but actively encouraging and participating in acts of love and service within our community. We believe in putting our faith into action through various outreach programs and community development initiatives.

Adopt a Family Initiative

Our Bishop, as the vision bearer for the Adopt a Family initiative, leads us in enhancing love through evangelism that requires vigorous follow-ups on families and friends.

"By this everyone will know that you are my disciples, if you love one another." (John 13:35)

This powerful scripture reminds us that our love for one another is the most visible testimony of our faith. Through this initiative, we practically demonstrate Christ's love by committing to walk alongside families, providing spiritual support, encouragement, and meeting practical needs as we're able.
